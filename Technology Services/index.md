# Technology Services

## Build

**Define Your Core Services:** Identify the fundamental technology services that underpin all other IT functions (e.g., Network, Storage, Hosting, Identity).

**Establish Service Ownership:** Assign a clear owner for each core technology service who is responsible for its reliability, performance, and cost.

**Create a Technology Roadmap:** Develop a high-level plan for the future of your key technologies, including major upgrades and replacements.

**Document Your Architecture:** Create diagrams and documents that show how your core technology services are designed and connected.

## Manage

**Focus on Reliability:** Make the stability and reliability of your core services your highest priority.

**Monitor Health and Capacity:** Keep a close watch on the performance and capacity of your infrastructure to prevent outages.

**Manage the Lifecycle:** Have a clear plan for maintaining, upgrading, and eventually retiring all your infrastructure components.

**Control Costs:** Understand the costs of your technology services and constantly look for ways to be more efficient.

## Maturity

- [ ] Can you name the owner for your network service? Your storage service?
- [ ] Do you have a technology roadmap that looks out at least 1-2 years?
- [ ] Do you have up-to-date diagrams of your core infrastructure?
- [ ] Do you know how much your core technology services cost the business each month?
