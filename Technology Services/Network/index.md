# Network

## Build

**Map Your Network:** Create and maintain an accurate diagram of your entire network, including all switches, routers, firewalls, and circuits.

**Standardize Your Configurations:** Develop standard, secure configuration templates for all your network devices.

**Segment Your Network:** Divide your network into different zones (e.g., a corporate zone, a guest zone, a data center zone) to limit the spread of attacks.

**Define Network Services:** Clearly document the network services you offer, such as VPN access, new office connectivity, or firewall rule changes.

## Manage

**Monitor Everything:** Use monitoring tools to watch the health, performance, and utilization of all your network links and devices.

**Manage Changes Carefully:** Follow a strict change management process for all network changes to avoid causing accidental outages.

**Keep Firmware Updated:** Have a process for regularly updating the software on your network devices to patch security holes.

**Plan for Capacity:** Constantly monitor bandwidth usage and plan for future upgrades before you run into performance problems.

## Maturity
