# Governance & Risk Management

## Build

**Define Your Policies:** Create a set of clear, simple rules for how the company will manage cybersecurity and risk.

**Identify Your Risks:** Make a list of the top security risks that could impact the business.

**Establish a Risk Committee:** Create a group of leaders who are responsible for making decisions about risk and security.

**Choose a Framework:** Adopt a recognized security framework (like NIST CSF or ISO 27001) to structure your program.

## Manage

**Conduct Regular Risk Assessments:** Periodically review your list of risks to see what's changed and what's new.

**Track Your Risks:** Maintain a risk register to track each identified risk, its owner, and the plan to address it.

**Report to Leadership:** Keep senior leadership informed about the company's top risks and the status of the security program.

**Stay on Top of Compliance:** Monitor your adherence to relevant regulations and standards.
