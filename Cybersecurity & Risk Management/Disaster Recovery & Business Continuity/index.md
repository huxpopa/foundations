# Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity

## Build

**Identify Critical Systems:** Make a list of the essential technology and business processes the company needs to survive.

**Perform a Business Impact Analysis (BIA):** Understand how much it would cost the business if each critical system went down.

**Create a Recovery Plan:** For each critical system, write down the step-by-step plan to get it back online after a disaster.

**Define Roles and Responsibilities:** Clearly assign who is responsible for what during a disaster recovery event.

## Manage

**Test Your Plans:** Regularly practice your recovery plans to make sure they work and that everyone knows their role.

**Keep It Updated:** Review and update your recovery plans at least once a year, or whenever a major system changes.

**Train Your Team:** Ensure that everyone involved in the recovery process is trained on the latest plans and procedures.

**Hold Post-Mortems:** After every test or actual event, meet to discuss what went well, what didn't, and how to improve.
