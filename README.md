# Welcome

This is the Foundation collection. A crowdsourced, community driven blueprint for building a strong foundation for your service oriented organization.  Focusing on the fundamentals over the complex.

## Rules
- Stay constructive and acknowledge we are not all perfect
- Keep it short and to the point
- Limit jargon or complex words
- Use links and sources inline and avoid disrupting simplicity
- Focus on content that is applicable to all types and sizes or organizations
