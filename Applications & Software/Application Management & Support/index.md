# Application Management & Support

## Build

**Know Your Apps:** Make a list of all the applications your company uses and what they do.

**Assign Owners:** For each application, make sure someone is responsible for it.

**Create a Support Plan:** Write down how to get help for each application, who to call, and what to do when things go wrong.

**Document Everything:** Keep a record of how each application works, who uses it, and any special instructions.

## Manage

**Regular Check-ins:** Have regular meetings with application owners to make sure everything is running smoothly.

**Track Problems:** Keep a list of any issues that come up and what's being done to fix them.

**Talk About Improvements:** Always be on the lookout for ways to make the applications better, faster, or easier to use.

**Keep Everyone Updated:** When you make changes, make sure everyone who uses the application knows what's new.

## Maturity

- [ ] Do you have a complete list of all your applications?
- [ ] Does every application have a clear owner?
- [ ] Is there a support plan for your most critical applications?
- [ ] Do you know who to contact for any application in your list?
